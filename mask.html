Introduction

Deep fakes are synthetic media that are indistinguishable from real content. This technology is becoming increasingly accessible and sophisticated, making it a potential threat to democracy and truth in the digital age.

Deep Fakes

A deep fake is a synthetic media that uses artificial intelligence to manipulate or generate audio, images, or videos. It's created using machine learning algorithms and can be used to impersonate someone else, alter existing content, or generate entirely new content.

Impact on Democracy

Deep fakes can have a significant impact on democracy by spreading false information, undermining trust in news and government institutions, and creating confusion and discord. It's important to be able to detect deep fakes and prevent their spread to protect democracy and truth in the digital age.

Prevention

There are several measures that can be taken to prevent the spread of deep fakes, such as:

  • Educating the public on how to spot deep fakes
  • Developing technical solutions to detect deep fakes
  • Regulating the creation and distribution of deep fakes
